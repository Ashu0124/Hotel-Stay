# 🏨 Hotel Management System (HMS)

A full-featured web-based Hotel Management System built to streamline hotel operations including room booking, guest management, billing, staff management, and reporting.

This application provides a centralized dashboard for administrators and staff to manage daily hotel operations efficiently and securely.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 🔐 Authentication & Role Management
- Secure Login / Logout
- Role-Based Access Control (Admin, Receptionist, Manager, Staff)
- Password Reset & Update
- Protected Routes

### 📊 Dashboard & Analytics
- Real-time Room Availability
- Occupancy Rate Overview
- Daily Check-ins / Check-outs
- Revenue Summary
- Visual Charts & Reports

### 🛏️ Room Management
- Add / Edit / Delete Rooms
- Room Categories (Standard, Deluxe, Suite)
- Room Status Tracking (Available, Occupied, Cleaning, Maintenance)
- Floor Management
- Amenities Assignment
- Seasonal Pricing

### 📅 Reservation Management
- New Booking Creation
- Modify / Cancel Booking
- Advance Reservations
- Walk-in Booking
- Guest Details Capture
- Special Request Management

### 🏷️ Check-In / Check-Out
- Express Check-in
- Late/Early Check-out Handling
- Auto Invoice Generation
- Digital Record Management

### 👤 Guest Management
- Guest Profile Creation
- Booking History Tracking
- Loyalty Tracking (Optional)
- Corporate Guest Management

### 💳 Billing & Payments
- Automatic Bill Calculation
- Tax (GST/VAT) Configuration
- Invoice Generation (Printable & Downloadable)
- Multiple Payment Methods:
  - Cash
  - Card
  - Online Payment
- Refund & Partial Payment Support

### 👨‍💼 Staff Management
- Add / Manage Staff
- Role Assignment
- Activity Tracking
- Shift Management

### 🧹 Housekeeping Management
- Cleaning Status Updates
- Maintenance Issue Reporting
- Room Service Status

### 📦 Inventory Management (Optional)
- Track Hotel Supplies
- Low Stock Alerts
- Vendor Management

### 📈 Reports
- Revenue Reports
- Occupancy Reports
- Daily Sales Report
- Tax Reports
- Export to Excel / PDF
